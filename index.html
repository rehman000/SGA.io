SGA - SciFi Gaming and Animation

What is SGA?

The Sci-Fi, Gaming and Animation Club exists to provide CCNY students a place where science fiction, games, and animation are shared in an exciting and relaxing environment. It's a place where you can learn, grow, and share your knowlegde, thoughts, or feelings in a small group of fellow comrades. College can be tough and stressful, but SGA is here to ease things and provide entertainment, relaxation, and emotional support.

Game Nights Every Thursday

Game Nights are held every Thursday from 4:00 PM - 8:00 PM, in NAC 1/211. Each of these game nights have a unique theme, this weeks theme is Indie Night. Bring all the best Indie Games you have ever played, and share it with everyone! To find out more join the club discord server!
